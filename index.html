The Pink city
Jaipur

Jaipur also Known as "The Pink city" of Rajasthan. It is one of the most visited tourist destinations in Rajasthan. Jaipur is famous for its magnificent forts and places, local cuisines, wildlife and adventure tourism, pilgrimage sites and local shopping.

The city of Lakes
Udaipur

Udaipur, formarly the capital of the Mewar kingdom, is a city in the western Indian state of Rajasthan. Founded by Maharana Udai Singh in 1559, it's set around a series of artificial lakes and is known for its lavish royal residences.

The Golden City
Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, is the heart of Thar Desert. Known as the "Golden City", it's distinguished by its yellow sandstone architecture. The Jaisalmer Fort also known as Sonar Quila, is a UNESCO world heritage site and a major attraction in the city.

The Blue City
Jodhpur

Jodhpur, the second major city of Rajasthan is known as the Blue city as well as the Sun City of India. The City is acknowledge as the Sun City because it enjoys the bright and sunny weather all around the year and Blue city due to the blue-painted houses around the Mehrangarh Fort.

Heart Of Rajasthan
Ajmer

Ajmer is famous for the Dargah Sharif, which is the shrine of the sufi saint Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti. The city also has beautiful Ana Sagar Lake and Taragarh Fort, which stands high on a hill and provides panoramic view of the city.
